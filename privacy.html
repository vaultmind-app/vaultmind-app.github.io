Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

VaultMind ("we", "our", "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users save and organize AI conversations into Google NotebookLM notebooks. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Data Collection — What We Access

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

The extension accesses the following data, which stays entirely within your browser:

  • AI conversation content: When you explicitly click "Save to VaultMind," the extension reads the text content of the conversation visible on the current page (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, or Grok). This happens only when you initiate the save action.
  • Web page content: When you use the right-click "Save page to VaultMind" feature, the extension reads the text content of the current page. This happens only when you initiate the action.
  • Text selections: When you use the right-click "Save to VaultMind Notebook" feature on selected text, only the selected text is captured.

Data Storage

All data — including notebooks, sources, annotations, settings, and version history — is stored locally in your browser using the chrome.storage.local API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server owned or operated by VaultMind
  • Is not accessible to other extensions or websites
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Third-Party Services

Stripe (Payment Processing): If you choose to upgrade to a paid plan (Pro or Team), payment is handled by Stripe via ExtensionPay. When you make a payment, you interact directly with Stripe's checkout page. We do not see, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank details, or other financial information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy

ExtensionPay: ExtensionPay is used to manage paid subscriptions. It stores your email address and paid status on its servers to verify your subscription across devices. ExtensionPay's privacy policy: extensionpay.com/privacy

Google Fonts: The extension's injected UI loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies to font loading: policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions, each for a specific purpose:

  • storage: Store your notebooks, sources, annotations, and settings locally in the browser.
  • activeTab: Read the content of the current page when you use the "Save page" context menu action.
  • sidePanel: Display the VaultMind dashboard as a Chrome side panel.
  • alarms: Run scheduled auto-capture checks at configured intervals.
  • notifications: Show confirmation messages when content is saved and alert you to detected conflicts.
  • contextMenus: Add "Save to VaultMind" to the right-click menu.
  • scripting: Extract page content when you use the "Save page" context menu action.

The extension also requires host permissions for specific AI chat websites (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, perplexity.ai, notebooklm.google.com) to inject the "Save to VaultMind" button into those pages.

Data We Do NOT Collect

To be explicit, VaultMind does NOT:

  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect analytics or telemetry data
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels
  • Sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties
  • Store any data on our servers (we don't operate servers)
  • Access your data unless you explicitly trigger a save action
  • Read conversations on pages you don't save

Google API Services User Data Policy

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Children's Privacy

VaultMind is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
